Projects

MVE-VR

An all-inclusive training solution for vehicle inspections made for the Iowa Department of Transportation that I served as a developer on.

With MVE-VR, put your trainees through a level VI vehicle inspection and immediately conduct a segmented After Action Review. This review breaks down every portion of the vehicle inspection and allows you and your trainee to view an interactive simulation playback from different camera angles of what the trainee saw whilst in Virtual Reality.

In this full scale vehicle inspection simulator trainees can:

  • Interact with the driver and review their documents
  • Check lights and vehicle functions
  • Gather radiation readings of shipments
  • Inspect items and view their statuses
  • Go inside the cab and inspect instruments
  • Get under the hood and view critical parts
  • Take pictures and audio recordings of your findings

Before and after an inspection Instructors are able to:

  • Author their own vehicle damage generator for a more direct inspection
  • Put trainees in a completely randomized or a previously authored inspection
  • Spectate an inspection that is being conducted on another computer
  • Add users that need to conduct inspections
  • Review past inspections with a detailed segmented After Action Review

I personally developed:
